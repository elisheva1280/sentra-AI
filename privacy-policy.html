Sentra AI Mobile App

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when customers use the Sentra AI mobile app for camera alert and security-system management.

Last Updated: May 4, 2026. This Privacy Policy describes how Sentra AI handles information when you use the mobile app. For account deletion instructions, see the Account Deletion Request page.

Introduction

Sentra AI provides a customer-facing mobile application for managing connected NVR and camera alert systems. The app lets customers sign in, view connected NVR cameras, manage linked cameras, configure camera alert behavior, set mute schedules, choose detection classes and mask areas, view alert information, and verify a WhatsApp number for alert notifications.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sentra AI mobile app and the customer-facing services that support it. It does not cover separate admin, operator, or internal tools except where those systems are used to provide or support the mobile app service.

Information We Collect

  • Account and login data: email address, credentials submitted during login (processed via AWS Cognito, not stored by the application), customer/user ID, display name, organization/account ID, and active user selection. The app also uses email for password reset flows.
  • Authentication and session data: AWS Cognito user identifiers, access/ID tokens, refresh tokens, token refresh state, and session data. The mobile app stores the active session in the device secure keychain.
  • Phone and WhatsApp data: a WhatsApp phone number when the customer voluntarily enters one to set up alert notifications, a WhatsApp link token, the destination WhatsApp instance phone returned by the backend, and confirmation status received after verification. WhatsApp verification is initiated entirely by the user — the app does not access contacts, send unsolicited messages, or submit the phone number to WhatsApp without explicit user action.
  • Camera and NVR connection data: NVR/site names, manufacturer, model, serial number, device type, IP address, secondary IP address when provided, HTTP/RTSP ports, device username and password required to connect to the NVR, channels, interested channels, linked camera IDs, camera names, camera type, device/network/access/alert status, organization ID, client ID, and setup draft progress. This connection data is stored on the Sentra AI backend and used solely to operate, connect, and manage the customer's NVR and camera system.
  • Alert, detection, and configuration data: notification and event records, alert messages, event times, NVR event times, severity/status, viewed/resolved metadata, detection classes, selected detection class IDs, confidence values, bounding boxes, tags, mute state, temporary inactive periods, weekly mute schedules, time zones, and camera mask polygons or regions configured by the customer.
  • Camera images, snapshots, and event media: the app can load and display camera preview image URLs and last-snapshot URLs returned by the backend. Notification and event records also include snapshot image IDs, temporary image URLs, and event/video media identifiers where available. The app does not request access to the phone camera, microphone, contacts, or local photo library.
  • Operational and diagnostic data: technical information generated during API communication, including request metadata, timestamps, error types, authentication errors, and connection error details. These logs are used solely for troubleshooting, security, and service reliability. The app does not use advertising technologies, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Data Safety Summary

The categories below summarize types of information the service may collect or process depending on how you use Sentra AI. This summary aligns app store data safety disclosures with this policy.

  • Account information: email address, account/user IDs, display name, organization ID
  • Phone / WhatsApp number: only if you use the WhatsApp alert-notification feature
  • Camera and NVR configuration: device connection details, camera settings, detection preferences, schedules, mute windows, and masks
  • Snapshots and event media: preview URLs, event image identifiers, and temporary media URLs where available
  • App interactions and settings: in-app navigation, configuration changes, and feature usage
  • Operational and diagnostic data: API request metadata, error types, timestamps, and related technical logs used for troubleshooting and security

We do not sell personal information, use data for advertising purposes, or engage in cross-app tracking. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics profiling tools, or cross-device tracking mechanisms.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users, maintain sessions, and protect account access.
  • Create, link, or validate customer profiles and organization access.
  • Display customer NVRs, cameras, linked cameras, camera status, and camera preview images.
  • Connect, scan, activate, sync, and manage NVRs and related camera channels.
  • Save customer configuration choices, including linked cameras, interested channels, detection classes, camera masks, mute schedules, temporary mute windows, and NVR setup drafts.
  • List and display notifications, alerts, event records, snapshots, and available event media.
  • Send WhatsApp alert notifications to the phone number the user explicitly verified — only when the user initiates the WhatsApp verification flow.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the reliability of the app and supporting services.
  • Respond to support inquiries, including NVR setup and connection diagnostics submitted by the customer.

Sharing With Service Providers

We do not sell personal information, use data for advertising purposes, or engage in cross-app tracking. We share information with service providers only as necessary to operate, secure, and support the app and alert service.

  • AWS Cognito: processes user authentication credentials, password reset flows, and issues the tokens used to authorize API requests. User account passwords are managed entirely by Cognito and are not stored or processed by the Sentra AI application itself.
  • Sentra AI backend API (api.x-sentral.com): processes customer profile, camera, NVR, notification, schedule, detection, mask, event, WhatsApp-linking, and related operational data as described in this policy.
  • WhatsApp / Green API: when you choose to set up WhatsApp alert notifications, the app opens WhatsApp with a pre-prepared message. Your phone number is only submitted as part of this flow when you explicitly initiate it. The WhatsApp verification is entirely user-initiated — no phone number is shared without your direct action.
  • AWS S3 (cloud media storage): camera snapshots, preview URLs, temporary media URLs, and event media files may be stored in or served from AWS S3 buckets managed as part of the Sentra AI platform.
  • NVR and camera integration services: camera and NVR connection details are processed by backend services that connect to your NVR device to retrieve device status, channel lists, snapshots, and alert data.

Data may be processed in countries where these service providers operate. Where applicable laws require safeguards for international data transfers, we rely on contractual and organizational measures consistent with those requirements.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

  • Authentication: all API requests require a valid bearer token issued by AWS Cognito using RS256 JWT signing. Access is enforced by role-based authorization (customer, operator, admin).
  • Data in transit: all communication between the mobile app and Sentra AI APIs uses HTTPS (TLS). The production API endpoint is served exclusively over HTTPS.
  • Session storage: session tokens on the mobile device are stored in the platform's secure keychain (or equivalent secure credential storage).
  • Sensitive device data: NVR connection credentials, IP addresses, ports, and camera configuration are treated as sensitive operational data. Access is restricted to authorized system services and personnel required to operate, support, or maintain the service. This data is not logged in plaintext in diagnostic outputs.
  • User account credentials: user login passwords are managed entirely by AWS Cognito and are never received, stored, or processed by the Sentra AI application backend.

We do not claim application-level encryption at rest across all data subsystems, as storage-level protections depend on infrastructure configuration. No security measure is completely foolproof — no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. This policy does not claim HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO, or GDPR certification.

Data Retention

We retain different categories of data for different periods depending on operational, security, legal, and contractual requirements. In general:

  • Account and profile data is retained while the account is active and for a period after deletion sufficient to satisfy security, contractual, legal, or billing obligations.
  • NVR and camera configuration data is retained while the device is associated with the account and may remain in logs or backup records for operational or security purposes after removal.
  • Alert, event, and notification records may be retained for security review, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes beyond the lifetime of an active alert.
  • Diagnostic and operational logs are retained for the period needed to troubleshoot issues, investigate incidents, and maintain platform security and integrity.
  • WhatsApp verification metadata is retained while the number is linked and may remain in operational records after it is unlinked.
  • Support inquiries are retained for the period needed to resolve the inquiry and for any applicable record-keeping obligations.

Specific retention durations are not published here because they depend on the nature of the data and applicable legal, contractual, or operational requirements. We do not retain data longer than necessary for these purposes.

User Rights and Deletion Requests

Where applicable privacy laws grant rights, customers may request access, correction, deactivation, or deletion of personal information associated with the account, subject to verified identity where required and lawful exceptions (including retention needed for security, legal obligations, contractual or billing obligations, dispute resolution, or continued operation of our services).

How to request account deletion:

  • In the Sentra AI mobile app, open Settings → Delete Account while signed in — this submits a routed deletion request through your authenticated profile.
  • Use the public Account Deletion Request page or email workflow.
  • Email support@app-bizz.com, including the email address you use to sign in.

Processing timeframe. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law, security obligations, contractual requirements, billing records, dispute resolution, or operational necessity.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.
