Sentral AI Mobile App

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when customers use the Sentral AI React Native mobile app for camera alert and security-system management.

Last Updated: May 4, 2026. This Privacy Policy describes how Sentral AI handles information when you use the mobile app. For account deletion instructions, see the Account Deletion Request page.

Introduction

Sentral AI provides a customer-facing mobile application for managing connected NVR and camera alert systems. The app lets customers sign in, view connected NVR cameras, manage linked cameras, configure camera alert behavior, set mute schedules, choose detection classes and mask areas, view alert information, and verify a WhatsApp number for alert notifications.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sentral AI mobile app and the customer-facing services that support it. It does not cover separate admin, operator, or internal tools except where those systems are used to provide or support the mobile app service.

Information We Collect

  • Account and login data: email address, credentials submitted during login (processed securely via authentication provider), customer/user ID, display name, organization/account ID, and active user selection. The app also uses email for password reset flows.
  • Authentication and session data: AWS Cognito user identifiers, access/ID tokens, refresh tokens, token refresh state, and session data. The mobile app stores the active session in the device secure keychain.
  • Phone and WhatsApp data: a WhatsApp phone number when the customer enters one for verification, a WhatsApp link token, the destination WhatsApp instance phone returned by the backend, and confirmation status received after verification.
  • Camera and NVR data: NVR/site names, manufacturer, model, serial number, device type, IP address, secondary IP address when provided, HTTP/RTSP ports, username, password, channels, interested channels, linked camera IDs, camera names, camera type, device/network/access/alert status, organization ID, client ID, and setup draft progress.
  • Alert, detection, and configuration data: notification and event records, alert messages, event times, NVR event times, severity/status, viewed/resolved metadata, detection classes, selected detection class IDs, confidence values, bounding boxes, tags, mute state, temporary inactive periods, weekly mute schedules, time zones, and camera mask polygons or regions configured by the customer.
  • Camera images, snapshots, and event media: the app can load and display camera preview image URLs and last-snapshot URLs returned by the backend. Notification and event DTOs also include snapshot image IDs, temporary image URLs, and event/video media identifiers where available. The current Android app manifest does not request access to the phone camera, microphone, contacts, or photos.
  • Device, network, and diagnostics data: request metadata needed for API communication, timestamps, network errors, authentication errors, connection errors, and development or server-side diagnostic and security logs associated with operating the platform. The app does not use advertising technologies, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or cross-app tracking mechanisms.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users, maintain sessions, and protect account access.
  • Create, link, or validate customer profiles and organization access.
  • Display customer NVRs, cameras, linked cameras, camera status, and camera preview images.
  • Connect, scan, activate, sync, and manage NVRs and related camera channels.
  • Save customer configuration choices, including linked cameras, interested channels, detection classes, camera masks, mute schedules, temporary mute windows, and NVR setup drafts.
  • List and display notifications, alerts, event records, snapshots, and available event media.
  • Verify WhatsApp numbers and send customers to WhatsApp to complete alert-notification setup.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the reliability of the app and supporting services.
  • Respond to support inquiries, including NVR setup and connection diagnostics submitted by the customer.

Sharing With Service Providers

We do not sell personal information or use the mobile app code to add advertising or cross-app tracking. We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate, secure, and support the app and alert service.

  • AWS Cognito: used for authentication, password reset, token issuance, and token refresh.
  • Sentral backend API: the production mobile app communicates with https://api.x-sentral.com to load and update customer profile, camera, NVR, notification, schedule, detection, mask, event, and WhatsApp-linking data.
  • WhatsApp: when WhatsApp verification is used, the app opens WhatsApp with a verification token and the backend processes the result so alert notifications can be delivered to the verified number.
  • Cloud storage and media services: camera snapshots, preview URLs, temporary media URLs, and event media may be processed or stored by backend/cloud services that support the Sentral alert platform.
  • NVR and camera integration services: camera and NVR connection details may be processed by backend services that connect to supported NVR devices and retrieve device, channel, status, snapshot, or alert information.

In plain language, how providers fit together:

  • AWS Cognito processes authentication data and identifiers needed to operate sign-in, password reset, and token issuance.
  • The Sentral backend processes app, account, NVR/camera, configuration, alert, WhatsApp linking, and related operational data described in “Information We Collect.”
  • WhatsApp may receive your phone number and the verification-related content when you initiate verification from the app.
  • Cloud storage and media services may process snapshots, preview URLs, temporary media URLs, and event or alert-related media identifiers as part of the platform.

Data may be processed in countries where service providers operate. Where applicable law requires safeguards for international transfers, we use contractual and organizational measures that align with legal requirements relevant to how we operate the service.

Data Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. Authentication is handled through AWS Cognito, authenticated API requests use bearer tokens, and the mobile app stores session data in the device secure keychain.

Sensitive operational and security data. NVR credentials, IP addresses and ports, camera/NVR configuration, and snapshots or event-related media handled by the platform are treated as sensitive. Access is limited to authorized systems and personnel as needed to operate, support, maintain security, troubleshoot, protect customers, and ensure platform integrity.

Data transmitted between the mobile app and Sentral APIs is protected in transit using HTTPS (TLS). Session tokens are stored using the mobile platform’s secure credential storage capabilities (secure keychain or equivalent). We do not state here that all information is uniformly encrypted at rest in every subsystem; safeguards depend on infrastructure and retention context.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. This policy does not claim independent security certification, HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO, or GDPR certification.

Data Retention

We retain account, authentication, device, configuration, alert, media, and diagnostic information for as long as needed to provide the service, keep customer security records, troubleshoot issues, comply with legal or contractual obligations, and protect the integrity of the platform.

Some information, such as alert history, snapshots, event media, audit or diagnostic logs, WhatsApp verification or delivery metadata, NVR setup drafts, and support inquiries, may be retained after a session ends or after a device is disconnected when needed for security, operational, legal, billing, or dispute-resolution purposes.

Retention periods depend on the type of data and operational requirements.

User Rights and Deletion Requests

Where applicable privacy laws grant rights, customers may request access, correction, deactivation, or deletion of personal information associated with the account, subject to verified identity where required and lawful exceptions (including retention needed for security, legal obligations, contractual or billing obligations, dispute resolution, or continued operation of our services).

How you can request account deletion: (1) In the Sentral AI mobile app, open Settings → Delete Account while signed in; (2) use our public Account Deletion Request page or email workflow; or (3) email support@app-bizz.com, including the email address you use to sign in. Organizations that manage tenant access should also coordinate with internal administrators where applicable.

Processing timeframe. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law, security, contractual, billing, dispute-resolution, or operational obligations.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.
