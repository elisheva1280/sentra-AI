Account Deletion Request

You may request deletion of your Sentral AI account and related personal data by following the steps below.

Processing time. Account deletion is not automatic or immediate. We review requests and handle them according to our operational process.
Identity verification. Support may verify your identity before processing a deletion request to protect your account.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to support@app-bizz.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Account deletion request — Sentral AI (copy it exactly so your request is routed correctly).
  3. In the body of the email, include the email address you use to sign in to Sentral AI.
  4. If available, include any relevant organization or account details that help us locate your account (for example, organization name or context from your service agreement).

Why this page does not submit through the app API

This public web page does not call the Sentral backend. The platform inquiry endpoint used inside the authenticated app requires a signed-in user token, and browser security (CORS) is not configured for static hosting on GitHub Pages. Use email or phone below so your request reaches support reliably.

What may be deleted

Depending on your account and applicable requirements, we may delete or anonymize, where legally and operationally possible:

  • Account and profile data
  • Login-related account records
  • Camera and NVR configuration
  • Linked cameras
  • Alert settings
  • Schedules and mute windows
  • WhatsApp verification data
  • Support inquiries, where legally and operationally possible

What may be retained

Some information may be kept after account deletion, including where required or permitted by law or legitimate business needs, such as:

  • Security logs and audit records
  • Legal, contractual, or billing records
  • Diagnostic logs
  • Alert and event records needed for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, compliance, or platform integrity

Retained data is kept only as long as necessary for legal, security, operational, or contractual reasons.
